About ProgressWatch

Empowering citizens with transparent, real-time tracking of public interest projects, promises, and civic initiatives. Our mission is to promote accountability and foster trust in public service.

Our Purpose

ProgressWatch was founded on the belief that transparency and accountability are the cornerstones of a thriving democracy. We provide a platform for citizens to monitor the progress of public projects, track political promises, and stay informed about civic initiatives that impact their communities.

Our platform aggregates data from multiple sources, presents it in an accessible format, and encourages public participation. We are committed to making information open, accurate, and actionable for everyone.

Our Values

Transparency: We believe in open access to information.
Accountability: We hold public officials and projects to high standards.
Community: We empower citizens to engage and make a difference.

How the Projects & Cases Dashboard Works

The Projects and Cases dashboards are powerful tools designed to provide real-time insights into public interest projects and legal cases across South Africa. These dashboards aggregate data from multiple sources, allowing users to filter, search, and analyze information by province, municipality, category, status, and date range.

  • Projects Dashboard: Track the progress, budget, contractors, and milestones of infrastructure, education, healthcare, and climate projects nationwide. Visual charts and key stats help you quickly identify trends and top performers.
  • Cases Dashboard: Monitor high-profile legal cases, filter by status or category, and view details about plaintiffs, outcomes, and timelines. The dashboard highlights top plaintiffs and provides analytics on case distribution and resolution.

Benefits

  • Promotes transparency and accountability in public service delivery.
  • Empowers citizens, journalists, and officials with actionable data.
  • Enables early detection of delays, budget overruns, or legal bottlenecks.
  • Fosters community engagement and informed decision-making.
